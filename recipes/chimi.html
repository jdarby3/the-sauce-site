Argentine Chimmichurri

The Dish:

This chimichurri goes great on top of any grilled meat, steak or octopus are my favorites.

The Ingredients:

  • Fresh Parsley
  • Fresh or Dried Oregano
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

To make:

Take one bunch of parsley from the grocery store and chop the leaves to to a size of your preference, most likely the smaller the better. Then mince one clove of garlic and combine this with the parsley in a bowl. Add olive oil based on your consistency preference. Add the rest of the ingredients to taste, but be careful with the red wine vinegar it is strong!
