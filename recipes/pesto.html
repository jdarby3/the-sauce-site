Fresh Basil Pesto

The Dish:

This pesto has many different uses, from pasta to sandwiches to simply eating it on its own! It is very quick and easy to make, taking only about 5-10 minutes. Be sure to use very fresh basil as well as high quality parmesean and olive oil for the best results!

The Ingredients:

  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesean
  • Pine Nuts
  • Garlic
  • Salt

To Make:

For enough pesto to cover one pound of pasta use three ounces of basil, one clove of garlic and one cup of olive oil. Simply combine these in a food processor adding the rest of the ingredients to taste! Add more olive oil based on your consistency preference.
