Simple Hummus

The Dish:

This hummus goes great as a dip with fresh veggies or pita chips!

The Ingredients:

  • Garbanzo Beans
  • Olive Oil
  • Tahini
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt

To Make:

Blend together one can of garbanzo beans and two tablespoons of olive oil in a food processor. Add the rest of the ingredients to taste. Add more olive oil based on your consistency preference. Top with some paprika when it is time to serve!
